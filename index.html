Welcome to Firework Show Creator!

This is a website where you can customize a firework simulation with a variety of factors such as color, rate of fire, and sound volume. There is also a frames per second controller that allows you to choose which display is best suited for your target machine.

You can easily choose which colors you wish to include in your show by checking the box next to each color. Feel free to mix and match colors to fully personalize your experience!

Next, you can adjust the rate of fire by selecting from a number of options in the dropdown menu. If the sound effects get annoying or too loud with a faster rate of fire, feel free to use the slider next to the option to control the audio.

Lastly, if you want to sacrafice framerate for a more consistent speed, use the dropdown menu to select how many updates per second you would like. If you feel your computer can handle even more, there is also a 120FPS option for even smoother animations.

I hope you enjoy customizing your firework show!
